Dolmachi Burger Press

EASY TO USE

Simply add a scoop of burger mixture to the base mold and press. It’s suitable for making all types of burger patties in the same size quickly and easily. You can make 1/4 to 1/3 lb burger patty. Dolmachi Press is also dishwasher safe and easy to clean.

Ergonomic Handle Design

Dolmachi Press comes with a soft, round-shaped, and good-grip handlethat fits comfortably in the palm of your hand; making the pressing comfortable and painless.

Heavy Duty Press

Dolmachi Press is made of heavy duty quality materials; ensuring high performance and extremely durable press tool. The food-grade aluminum cast with non-stick polished surface will not rust, providing lifetime satisfaction. The hard plastic handle is detachable and heat-resistant.

+ 100 Wax Papers

Dolmachi Burger Press package includes 100 nonstick patty papers that you can use to make it easier to get the patties out of the aluminum base mold.
